Embracing Cloud Native Landscape in Drupal Development

Mais de 70 Drupalistas juntaram-se em Évora em Maio de 2024. Mais edições nos próximos anos !

Speaker: Ricardo Amaro

Level: Intermediate

What problem are we trying to solve?
The challenge of modernizing Drupal applications to leverage the  scalability, resilience, and flexibility of cloud-native architectures.

Why does it matter to the audience?
Drupal developers and site builders looking to improve performance,  reduce costs, and enhance the reliability of their applications will  benefit from understanding how cloud-native principles can be applied to  Drupal.

What are the key takeaways from the session?
Understanding the core principles of cloud-native development.
Practical steps to refactor a Drupal application for a cloud-native environment.
Tools and practices for monitoring and maintaining cloud-native Drupal applications.

Level of experience required: Intermediate to Advanced. Familiarity with Drupal and basic cloud concepts is recommended.
